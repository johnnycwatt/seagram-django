About Seagram Properties

A Legacy of Trust and Excellence Since 1825

Our History

In 1825, amidst the rolling hills of Silvermoon Village, Peter Seagram founded Seagram Properties with a vision to help families find their forever homes. What began as a humble endeavor in a quaint village office has grown into a cornerstone of real estate in the lands of Silvermoon Village, Riverwood, and Mystic Town.

Peter’s passion for community and his unwavering commitment to integrity laid the foundation for a legacy that would span over two centuries. Through wars, economic shifts, and the rise of modern technology, Seagram Properties has remained a steadfast partner to generations of families, helping them navigate the ever-changing landscape of real estate.

Today, under the leadership of Darian Seagram, Peter’s great-great-great-grandson, we continue to honor our founder’s vision. Darian brings a modern perspective while preserving the values that have defined us for 200 years. From our historic office at 7 Moonshadow Street in Riverwood, we forge connections across communities, ensuring every client finds not just a house, but a home where dreams can flourish.

Our Mission

At Seagram Properties, we believe that every property holds a story, and every client deserves a journey that’s as unique as their dreams. Our mission is to be the key that unlocks your path to the perfect home—whether you’re buying your first cottage in Mystic Town or selling a cherished estate in Riverwood.

We’re more than a real estate agency; we’re a bridge between past and future, connecting families to the lands we’ve served for centuries. With every transaction, we aim to turn dreams into addresses, creating lasting memories in the heart of Silvermoon Village and beyond.
